Increase results

Thoughts & Key Takeaways

Be the developer’s friend

My experience with developers is that they often aren't designers or they'll not as comfortable making design decisions. To help, I made my designs as clear as possible, including specific values and explanations while my background in front-end design also came in handy. I was more than happy to build out ideas myself, which took the guesswork out of development.

The design team of one

I served as the sole designer, working closely with a single developer. It was a big challenge that required compromise. For example, in an early-stage organization, the classic design-thinking process was tough to implement, but I did my best to make it work. The reality, and beauty, was that I had to stay flexible and trust my instincts.
