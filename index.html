About

I’m a visual designer with a passion for UX and psychology. I like riding bikes, running, and collecting houseplants.

I'm a multi-platform designer for Strategic Communications at the University of Notre Dame in South Bend, IN.

I use a human-centered approach to make an impact in the lives of others through design. I enjoy being involved in every aspect of the creative process and strongly value team collaboration.
