My design process

01

Understanding the expectations

The first step I like to take is making sure everyone’s on the same page: clarifying the goals, strategy, deliverables, and timelines with the team or stakeholders.

02

Define the problem(s)

Collecting multiple perspectives often sparks valuable conversations and helps shape clear problem statements which defines direction on who’s affected, how, and why it matters.

03

Research and competitor analysis

I combine available data and research with competitor reviews but also dig into the “why” that data can often miss (motivations, needs, and emotional drivers).

04

Develop solution(s)

I always brainstorm individually, but I value team collaboration because it often sparks more diverse ideas. I take notes, sketch, and let ideas build off each other, exploring different directions before narrowing things down.

05

Create and refine concepts

I use wireframes and prototypes to visualize ideas, explore different solutions, and test concepts early which helps teams and stakeholders understand ideas, give feedback, and align on direction.

06

Design and implementation

The part I love most: bringing concepts to life. Translating ideas into polished, functional designs while refining interactions and visuals to create experiences that are both functional and human.

How I Work

Power in Minimalism

I strive to help users focus on their goals without getting distracted or overwhelmed and ultimately abandoning the task at hand. When designing I try to contain components that are relevant and provide a clear and visible path to navigate to and through other content. With more complex tasks, I work to break it down into smaller steps in order to decrease a users' cognitive load.

A Shared Understanding

With my background as a Web Designer and experience in front-end development, I have an understanding of how a developer thinks. Having this understanding helps me be more flexible when working within a multidisciplinary team as I can sympathize with the developers and can accurately communicate to them my visual ideas and needs more easily.
